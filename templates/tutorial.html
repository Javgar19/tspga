Travelling Salesman Routing

Solve the Travelling Salesman Problem for your particular configuration of locations

Set the locations

Click on the map to the add a waypoint. Each point you add will be taken into consideration when computing the optimal path. In order to delete one location you can simply click on them. The first waypoiny you add will be the starting and ending point of the route.

Solve the problem

Click on the "Compute Shortest Route" button to find the optimal path. This will run the genetic algorithm in the backend that will try to find an optimal solution. Keep in mind that the TSP is a very complex problem to solve for even a relatively small number of locations. You will notice an increase on the time required for the algorithm to finish as you increase the number of waypoints in the map.

Compare solutions

You can erase all the waypoints and paths in the map by clicking on the "Clear Map" button. As genetic algorithms have an heuristic nature, you will notice that runing it multiple times may result in different solutions. To compare them, the total distance of every path is displayed below the map, on the right side of the page.
